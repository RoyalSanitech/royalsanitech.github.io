About Us

Royal Sani-Tech Engineers & Contractors is a professional plumbing and sanitary contracting company established in 1997. We specialize in plumbing works, sanitary insulation, and core cutting services for residential, commercial, and institutional projects. With experienced supervisors and skilled workforce, we deliver reliable and high-quality plumbing solutions for major builders and institutions.
